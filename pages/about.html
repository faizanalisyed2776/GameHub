Our Gaming Blog Journey

2012

First Post Goes Live

Our founder Alex published the first GamePulse review - a 2000-word deep dive into Skyrim's mechanics that went viral in gaming forums.

2015

Team Expansion

We brought on our first full-time writers and introduced our signature "Frame Rate Friday" weekly feature series.

2018

Community Focus

Launched our Discord server and reader-submitted "Community Spotlight" section that now features weekly fan content.

2020

Video Content

Expanded into YouTube with our "Dev Diaries" series, interviewing indie developers about their creative process.

2023

Award-Winning

Named "Best Independent Gaming Blog" by the Global Games Media Awards for our innovative coverage.

Our Blogging Philosophy

Honest Reviews

We maintain complete editorial independence - no publisher relationships influence our scores or coverage.

Deep Dives

GamePulse Lore

Every great gaming blog accumulates its own legends over time. Here are some of ours:

The 72-Hour Review

When Elden Ring launched, our team pulled three consecutive all-nighters to deliver the most comprehensive day-one review in the industry, complete with performance analysis across 12 hardware configurations.

The Leak That Wasn't

Our speculative "Next-Gen Consoles" article in 2019 accidentally predicted the PS5's specs with uncanny accuracy, leading to a brief internet frenzy that we'd been leaked insider information.

Community Save File

Our readers collectively wrote and voted on decisions for our "Mass Effect Legendary Edition" playthrough, creating the most chaotic Paragon/Renegade hybrid Shepard imaginable.
